ANNIKA VERPLANCKE


Annika Verplancke is a teenager with a cause—the 14-year-old Belgian-American is a self-styled “dancer against cancer,” shows off her moves and moving the hearts of over 3,000 followers as on Instagram. In this video, the vivacious and talented dancer performs a beautiful and curious hybrid ballet/hip-hop routine (with hybrid costume to match), at the Youth America Grand Prix 2015 European semi-final in Paris, with choreography by Magali Berael and Fundi Omari, performed to the music "Heart Cry" by Drehz. Watch, and be amazed.

RAMYA


Ramya Kalathil is an Indian Dancer, who is working in Tamil and Malayalam television industry. She was born in Bangalore, Karnataka. Ramya made her dancing debut in the Tamil television dance show “Maanada Mayilada season 9” presented by Sanjeev and Keerthi. She was Third place winner of Maanada Mayilada season 9 and D3 D4Dance. Ramya given the best performences in Maanada Mayilada season 10 and got tittle winner in the season. She was one of the contestant in D3 D4Dance at Mazhavil Manorama. She has appeared in some well known television shows such as D3 D4Dance and Maanada Mayilada. Ramya currently doing one of the contestant in Dance Jodi Dance 2.o reality dance Sshow along with Deva in Zee Tamil
